Mission Statement

As Christians in the Body of Christ, we believe God expects us to set our sights toward Heaven by His grace and gift of salvation through His Son Jesus Christ. We believe our battle is not against flesh and blood, but against those entities influenced by spiritual forces of evil, i.e. Satan. For this reason, we accept the challenge to educate ourselves and others of worldly issues which have an impact on Christians. We do this by the help of the Holy Spirit, and we give all honor and glory to God.
